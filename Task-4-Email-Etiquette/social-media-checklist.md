<h1 align="center">Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist</h1>
<br>

| ✅ Do's (Responsible Use) | ❌ Don'ts (Risky Behavior) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Do** regularly review and update your privacy settings to control who sees your content. | **Don't** post complaining or negative comments about your professors, classmates, or employers. |
| **Do** use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase academic projects, volunteer work, and professional skills. | **Don't** post or share inappropriate content, such as illegal activities or excessive partying, as employers often search social media. |
| **Do** pause and think before you post; remember that the internet is practically forever, even if you delete something. | **Don't** overshare personal, sensitive information like your home address, class schedule, or phone number. |
| **Do** engage respectfully with others, even when you disagree with their opinions. | **Don't** engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or spreading unverified rumors and misinformation. |
| **Do** separate your personal and professional digital identities if necessary (e.g., a private Instagram and a public LinkedIn). | **Don't** rely entirely on social media for news without fact-checking the sources. |
